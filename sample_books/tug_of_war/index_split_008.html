“Oh look! Papa found my worm!” Billy chirped. “It looks like I’m going to get my breakfast first!” Billy opened his very large mouth expecting Papa to drop the whole worm in.

“Billy,” Mama Robin told him, “this worm is big enough for all of you. Papa and I will keep looking, but it takes quite a long time to fly across the park and back. You children will just have to share this worm until we get back.”
