Papa’s sharp eyes soon spotted the cat and could see he was nearly to where poor Billy was trembling in the grass. Papa let out a loud, angry cry and dived straight down at the cat and pecked his head and flapped his wings noisily.

Then Mama attacked the cat as well, swooping in for a quick peck and then escaping before the cat could catch her. The cat was so surprised by this sudden attack from the air that he raced off across the park.
