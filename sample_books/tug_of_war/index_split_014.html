“Oh no!” cried Cherry. “Look over there, coming across the lawn this way! It’s that big mean old tomcat that Mama and Papa and all their friends have to keep chasing away.”

“Poor Billy doesn’t see him yet, but that old cat will soon see Billy! We have got to do something!” Reddy said in his most determined voice.

“Oh dear, this doesn’t look too good. Let’s all pray for Billy!” said Chirpy. “Dear God, please keep Billy. Rescue him from that terrible tomcat, and somehow, help him to get back up here in the nest.”
