“Oh no!” squawked Reddy. “Look, Billy has fallen all the way to the ground. Is he hurt?”

“I don’t think so,” said Cherry. “See, he’s getting up and hopping around. But he can’t fly yet and he is too big for Mama and Papa to try to lift, so how will he ever get back in the nest?”

Their eyes scanned the woods in every direction to see if help could be found, but there was none.
