Mama and Papa Robin were getting ready to fly out to search for some breakfast for everyone. This morning everyone was extra hungry.

“Papa, can you bring me a nice, fat, juicy worm?” pleaded Billy.

“I’ll try, Billy,” said Papa Robin, “but it’s not always easy to find worms, because the ground is hard and dry now and the worms are hiding deep underground.”

“Oh, I don’t like worms,” whined Chirpy. “They’re too long and squiggly to swallow. Papa, can you just bring us some bugs?”
